Connectivity-Score

We calculated the Connectivity-Score of two wards as follows:
The total of all the trips that run directly (zero-hops) from every stop in ward x to every stop in ward y, normalized by the product of the number of stops in ward x & ward y, is the connectivity score of a ward y with regard to a particular ward x.

Overlap Percentage

We calculated the Overlap-Score of two routes as follows:
Overlap of route x with route y is equal to the number of common stops divided by the number of stops on route x.
Overlap [Route x] [Route y] = Common Stops / Length (Route x)
Overlap [Route y] [Route x] = Common Stops / Length (Route y)
